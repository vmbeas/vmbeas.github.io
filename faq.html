  1. What is the StrongBuilt System? How is it made? What is it made of?

    2. The StrongBuilt System is a patented, high performance concrete building system. This extremely strong structural system consists of a super-insulated core of rigid expanded polystyrene sandwiched between two-engineered sheets of steel welded wire fabric mesh. To complete the panel form process a nine-gauge galvanized steel truss wire is pierced completely through the polystyrene core, and welded to each of the outer layer sheets steel welded wire fabric mesh. We then cast our proprietory, high performance concrete mix on both sides of the foam panels. This creates an airtight, and high strength system that is used to monolthically cast the walls and ceilings of a structure. The roofs can also be made using the system
